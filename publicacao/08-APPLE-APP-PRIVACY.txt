=== DATA LINKED TO YOU ===

| Data Type        | Usage             |
|------------------|-------------------|
| Email Address    | App Functionality |
| Precise Location | App Functionality |
| Coarse Location  | App Functionality |

=== DATA NOT LINKED TO YOU ===

| Data Type        | Usage             |
|------------------|-------------------|
| Crash Data       | App Functionality |
| Performance Data | App Functionality |

=== DATA USED TO TRACK YOU ===
None (marcar "No")

=== DETALHES ADICIONAIS ===

Location:
- Collected for geofencing (automatic work time tracking)
- NOT used for tracking or advertising
- User can disable at any time
- Linked to user identity for audit trail purposes

Email:
- Used for authentication only
- NOT used for marketing or third-party sharing

Crash Data:
- Sent to Sentry for crash reporting
- No PII included
- Not linked to user identity

Audio:
- NAO DECLARAR como "collected" porque nao e armazenado
- Audio e processado em tempo real e deletado imediatamente
- Se Apple questionar: "Audio is processed in real-time for voice command
  transcription and immediately deleted. No audio data is stored."
